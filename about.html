About Us

At the Department of Education Region #10, we strive to ensure that every child embarks on their educational journey with the best possible start. Our mission is to match students with nursery schools that not only align with their unique needs and interests but are also conveniently located close to their homes. By leveraging our expertise and local knowledge, we aim to facilitate a smooth transition into early education, providing a nurturing and accessible environment for every child. We believe that a strong educational foundation begins with the right support and proximity, and we are dedicated to making that vision a reality for all families in our region.
